About us


Peak & Planting


Peak & Planting, established in 1955, started off by practicing corporate farming. With the mission to revolutionize the agriculture sector of Pakistan, Peak&Planting realized that there is a need to upgrade complete supply chain and aimed to bring all solutions under one roof.
Starting with farm mechanization, Peak&Planting takes pride in introducing the technology of greenhouses in Pakistan. It was Then backed up by the initiative of distributing hybrid seeds in Pakistan. The idea of drip irrigation was also first floated in by Peak&Planting. In order to save farmer’s time and enhance production, Peak&Planting recently set up a seedling production plant for mass distribution. Pesticides and post-harvest technologies are what Peak&Planting is next venturing into.







“Meet the challenges of food security by maximising the returns of farmers through diversity and new technologies.”


Muhammad Yousaf Ejaz

Founder, Peak & Planting
